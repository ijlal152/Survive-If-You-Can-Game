Project scope: Survival games are considered an extension of a common video game theme where the player character is stranded or separated from others and must work alone to survive in a jungle and complete a goal. Game progressions: Progression gameplay is a game design term that refers to video game mechanics in which the designer sets a course of action that a player must complete to move forward in the game. Progression gameplay depends heavily on checkpoints that a character must reach to advance to the next level.
Some general checkpoints of this game include:
  • Energy level will rise up when player will find any energy booster.
  • Level of difficulty will be increase when a specific number of animals will be killed.
  • Player will get some extra points when any dangerous animal is killed.
